Overview

GetTakaful User Guide

A complete walkthrough of the GetTakaful Shariah-compliant cooperative insurance platform.

What is GetTakaful?

GetTakaful is a Shariah-compliant cooperative insurance platform that enables communities to collaboratively manage risk. Members form groups, pool their contributions, and collectively decide on claim payouts through a transparent voting system.

Key Features

Group Management

Create and manage Takaful groups with custom fees

Secure Payments

Stripe-powered payment processing for joining fees

Claims System

Submit claims with evidence and documentation

Democratic Voting

70% approval threshold for transparent claim decisions

Getting Started

Registration

To get started with GetTakaful, create a new account by following these steps:

1

Navigate to the Sign Up page

Click "Signup" on the login screen to open the registration form.

2

Fill in your details

Enter your First Name, Last Name, Email Address (must be unique), Phone Number (optional), and Password (minimum 8 characters).

3

Verify your email

You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) via email. Enter it to complete your registration.

Login

Access your account by entering your registered email and password.

1

Open the login page

Navigate to the application URL. The login page is the default landing page for unauthenticated users.

2

Enter your credentials

Type your Email and Password in the respective fields.

3

Click Login

Press the Login button. You will be redirected to the Dashboard upon success.

The GetTakaful login page with email and password fields.

Forgot your password? Click the "Forgot your password?" link on the login page. Enter your email and you will receive a reset link.

Creating a Group

As a group creator, you set the joining fee, minimum members, management fees, and terms for your Takaful group.

1

Click "+ Create New Takaful"

Available on the Dashboard or My Takaful page.

2

Fill in the group details

  • Takaful Name (required) - e.g., "Calgary Community Auto Takaful"
  • Description - Purpose of the group
  • Joining Amount (CAD) (required) - Fee each member pays
  • Minimum Members (required)
  • Management Fee, Claims Processing Fee, Shariah Review Fee, Platform Fee (optional)
  • Terms & Conditions - Rules governing the group
3

Submit

Click "Create Takaful". You are automatically added as the Admin.

2

Fill in the claim details

  • Date of Incident and Amount Claimed (required)
  • Location, type of incident, estimated repairs, description
  • Vehicle details, witness details, bank account info
3

Upload required evidence

Photos of Damage

Required

Required

Required

4

Submit the claim

The claim status is set to "Pending" and an email is sent to the group owner for review.

Owner Review

When a claim is submitted, the group owner must review and either approve it for voting or reject it with a reason.

Claim Workflow

Submitted Owner Review
Approved → Voting or Rejected (with reason)
70% Approved
1

Owner receives email notification

An email is sent to the group owner with a "Review Claim" link.

2

Review the claim details

The owner sees the full claim with evidence and an "Owner Review" form.

3

Make a decision

  • Approve for Voting - Emails all members to vote. 7-day voting window begins.
  • Reject - Must provide a reason. Claim status changes to "Rejected".

Voting System

After owner approval, all group members can vote to approve or deny a claim within the 7-day voting window.

Claim detail page showing the voting section, incident details, and evidence documents.

How to Vote

1

Navigate to the claim

Click the voting link in the email or navigate from the Dashboard's "Pending Votes" table.

2

Select your decision

Choose "Approve" or "Deny". Optionally add a comment.

3

Submit your vote

Click "Submit Vote". Your vote is recorded and cannot be changed.

Voting Rules

Every group member (except the claimant) can vote

One user = one vote. You cannot change your vote

The claimant cannot vote on their own claim

Voting window is 7 days after owner approval

Claim is approved only if at least 70% of eligible voters approve

Example: A group has 10 members. 1 is the claimant, so 9 are eligible voters. At least 7 members (70% of 9) must vote "Approve" for the claim to pass.

Voting Results

Any user visiting the claim detail page can see the voting results with a visual donut chart and threshold progress bar.

Claim detail page showing the "approved" status after reaching the 70% voting threshold.

Voting Result Components

Donut Chart

Visual breakdown of Approved (teal), Denied (red), and Not Voted (gray) with the approval percentage displayed in the center.

Statistics Panel

Shows approved votes, denied votes, and not-voted counts with percentages and color-coded indicators.

Threshold Progress Bar

A progress bar showing the current approval percentage against the 70% threshold, with a badge indicating whether the threshold has been met.

Notifications

Email Notifications

GetTakaful sends automated email notifications at every key step in the workflow.

Group Invitation Email

Trigger: When a member invites someone via "Invite Friends".

Content: GetTakaful logo, greeting, platform description, and join link.

Claim Submitted (to Owner)

Trigger: When a member submits a new claim.

Content: Claimant name, amount, date, policy number, and "Review Claim" button.

Voting Notification (to Members)

Trigger: When the group owner approves a claim for voting.

Content: Group name, claimant, amount, voting deadline, and "Vote Now" button.
